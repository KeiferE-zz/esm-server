How it Works

All accepted comments submitted to the Environmental Assessment Office (EAO) will be published online within seven days of receipt. Comments are not accepted if - in EAOâ€™s view - they are profane, abusive or do not relate to the matter being consulted upon as stated in our Public Comment Policy [PDF].

Comments related to matters of federal concern are forwarded to the Agency for review.

EAO requires that you provide your name and city/town when making a comment, but in order to have your name displayed with your comment; you must select the 'Make my Name visible to public' option. All hyperlinks in comments will be converted to plain text. All attachments must be no larger than 5MB.
